Our Services

  • Letter of Credit: A document issued by a bank that guarantees payment to a seller on behalf of a buyer.
  • Bank Guarantee and Tender Guarantee: A promise by a bank to cover a loss in case a borrower defaults on a loan.
  • Proof of Funds: A document or statement from a financial institution that verifies an individual or entity has a certain amount of money available.
  • Pre-Advice Services: Services offered by banks to inform the beneficiary of the details of a letter of credit before the shipping documents are presented.
  • Blocked Fund Services: Services that block a certain amount of funds in an account for a specific purpose.
  • Bank Comfort Letters: A document that assures a third party of the financial soundness of a particular individual or company.
  • Ready, Willing, and Able Letters: A document that confirms that an individual or company is financially capable of fulfilling a contract.
